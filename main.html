COLORS OF THE RAINBOW

(ROYGBIV)

Red Red is the first color of the rainbow. It has the longest wavelength. It represents wisdom and energy.

Orange Orange is the second color of the rainbow arch. Furthermore, this color is a combination of the color before and after it (Red and Yellow respectively). Besides, in a rainbow orange represents creativity and the ability to enjoy oneself.

Yellow Yellow is the third color of the rainbow arch. It corresponds to the brilliance of thoughts and wisdom and represents the brilliance of the sun.

Green Green is the fourth color of the rainbow. It represents nature, health, love, and wealth.

Blue Blue is the fifth color of the arch of the rainbow. It corresponds with water which represents purity.

Indigo Indigo is the sixth color of the rainbow arch. In addition, it represents the bridge between the subconscious and conscious worlds within the rainbow.

VIolet Violet is the last and seventh color of the rainbow which has the shortest wavelength. Within the rainbow, the color is a mixture of red and blue and it corresponds to divine inspiration and imagination.
